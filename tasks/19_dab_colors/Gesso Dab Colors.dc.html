Rules baked in
  • Every color is mid-to-deep enough that a white initial stays legible — no pale yellows or pastels that lose the letter.
  • All muted (editorial, not neon) so they harmonize on cream and with the ultramarine accent.
  • Default = ultramarine #2230b8. New players get a random one so boards look varied out of the box.
  • Dab shape cycles independently of color for extra variety on the board.
